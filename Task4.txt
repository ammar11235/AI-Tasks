1- DevOps Vs agile ?
	- Agile : is a software development methodology that emphasizes iterative development, continuous testing, and customer collaboration.
 	  Agile teams typically work in short sprints, and they release working software to customers frequently. 
	  This allows them to get feedback from customers early and often, and it helps to ensure that the software meets the needs of the users.

	- DevOps is a software development methodology that focuses on the collaboration between development, operations, and security teams.
	  DevOps teams work together to automate the delivery and deployment of software, and they strive to achieve continuous delivery and continuous deployment.
	  This allows them to release software more frequently and with fewer errors.
